Frequently Asked Questions

Time to ditch your typical interview process and enter the modern era of collecting feedback. AI Interviews ask a series of questions in conversational format, creating a personalized and engaging experience as AI asks follow up questions based on how users respond.

HyperMind is SOC II Type 2, ISO 27001 and GDPR compliant, with a HIPAA certification expected in February 2025. HyperMind gives you full control over the AI, including the data you use to train your custom AI and how AI is allowed to interact with your Users.

Anyone who needs to gather feedback from someone else is a customer of HyperMind AI. This can be marketers looking to understand customer preferences, sales teams who want to do win-loss analysis, product and design teams who want to validate ideas, human resources teams who want to collect employee feedback, engineering teams who want to understand the impact of tech debt on user experience, school administrators who want to collect student feedback, non-profit fundraisers who want to gather donor feedback, or even single people who want to get feedback on their dating profile.

HyperMind AI Conversations can be used to gather 4 kinds of feedback:
1) AI Surveys - a series of rating scale, multiple choice, ranking or open-ended questions.
2) AI Interviews - a conversation with Users that that goes in depth about their experiences and opinions.
3) AI Pulses - a single-question survey to take the pulse of Users (NPS, CSAT, CES, CPV).
4) AI Polls - supercharge your polls by asking users why they answered the way they did.

AI Surveys are an incredible upgrade to traditional surveys. Questions are asked of users in a chat-like format, and you can give AI permission to ask users follow up questions based on their response. This not only personalizes the conversation, but it also leads to much higher completion rates.
